Face Training

📚 Help & Workflow Guide

▼

Overview

The Face Training system helps you identify and organize videos by the people in them. Train profiles with positive and negative examples for accurate recognition.

Workflow

  • Step 1: Create person profiles with unique names and face tolerance settings (0.3-0.9, lower = stricter matching)
  • Step 2: Review "Unknown Person Videos" - these contain unrecognized faces
  • Step 3: Use video controls to navigate frame-by-frame and find clear face shots
  • Step 4: Click "Scan Video for Faces" to extract face crops from the current position (±15 frames)
  • Step 5: Select extracted faces and assign them as positive (is this person) or negative (not this person) examples
  • Step 6: Review sorted videos to correct any misidentified people

Features

  • Face Model: Choose HOG (faster, CPU) or CNN (more accurate, GPU) detection
  • Frame-Area Scanning: Extracts faces only from ±15 frames around your current video position for faster processing
  • Video Controls: Navigate frame-by-frame, adjust playback speed, and see current timestamp
  • Pagination: Browse videos in pages (10/25/50/100 per page) for faster loading
  • Bulk Actions: Select multiple videos to delete or reprocess at once
  • Negative Examples: Improve accuracy by marking faces that are NOT a specific person

Tips

  • Use lower face tolerance (0.4-0.5) for faces that look similar, higher (0.6-0.7) for varied lighting/angles
  • Pause on clear, front-facing shots before scanning for best results
  • Add both positive AND negative examples for each profile to reduce false positives
  • Use CNN model for challenging lighting or partial faces, HOG for speed
